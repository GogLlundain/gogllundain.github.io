Christ Church Fulham Christmas Choir 2024


The choir will perform 'O Holy Night' as a performance piece and support the other performers in singing a number other carols.

This is the running order of the songs; there will be prayers, readings, and a sermon sprinkled amongst them.

  1. O come, all ye faithful (sheet music)
  2. Joy to the world (sheet music, verses are on the final page)
  3. O holy night (sheet music and lyrics)
  4. The first noel
  5. Light of the world
  6. Hark! The herald angels sing (sheet music)
  7. Angels from the realms of glory
  8. Silent night (sheet music)
